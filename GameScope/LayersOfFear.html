Layers of Fear

Release year: 2016

Publisher: Bloober Team

Genre: horror, indie, puzzle

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"Layers of Fear" is an immersive psychological horror video game developed by Bloober Team. The game plunges players into a deeply unsettling atmosphere as they navigate the ever-changing Victorian mansion of a tormented painter. With a strong focus on story and exploration, players unravel the dark and poignant narrative of the artist's past, uncovering the tragedies that have affected his psyche and manifested into the eerie, shifting environment of the mansion. The gameplay is characterized by its use of perspective and optical illusions, which play with the player's sense of reality and create a disturbingly unpredictable experience. As players delve deeper into the layers of the artist's disturbed mind, they encounter intense visual storytelling, puzzles, and a haunting soundtrack that contribute to the game's chilling and introspective horror experience.
