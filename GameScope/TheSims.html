The Sims 4

Release year: 2014

Publisher: Electronic Arts

Genre: life simulation, character customization

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"The Sims 4" is a life simulation game developed by Maxis and published by Electronic Arts, marking the fourth major installment in the popular Sims franchise. Released in 2014, the game allows players to create and control characters known as "Sims" in various activities and form relationships, much like real life. The core appeal of "The Sims 4" lies in its incredibly detailed character customization options, enhanced building tools, and the deep emotional states of Sims that affect gameplay in nuanced ways. Over the years, numerous expansions and downloadable content packs have added depth and diversity to the game's world, introducing new themes, professions, and adventures. With its rich modding community and continual updates, "The Sims 4" maintains a vibrant gameplay experience that simulates life's complexities and joys in a uniquely interactive form.
