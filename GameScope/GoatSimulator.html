Goat Simulator

Release year: 2014

Publisher: Coffee Stain Studios

Genre: indie, adventure

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"Goat Simulator" is an offbeat, third-person action game developed by Coffee Stain Studios, renowned for its humorous and unconventional gameplay. Released in 2014, the game started as a joke prototype, but its popularity on social media led to a full release. In "Goat Simulator," players control a goat and are encouraged to explore the game's open world while causing as much destruction as possible. The game is known for its deliberately glitchy and physics-based mechanics that often result in unpredictable and hilarious outcomes. Objectives are loosely defined, with the main goal being to simply wreak havoc and collect points for performing various stunts and interactions within the environment. The game's absurdity is complemented by its parodic elements, mocking more serious simulation games. "Goat Simulator" has spawned several expansions and versions, including ones with zombies, space travel, and MMO-style gameplay, continuously adding to its chaotic and fun-filled universe.
