Dead by Daylight

Release year: 2016

Publisher: Behaviour Interactive

Genre: co-op, online

Rate(Steam): 9/10

"Dead by Daylight" is an asymmetric multiplayer horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive, released in 2016. The game features a thrilling hide-and-seek style gameplay where one player takes on the role of a killer, while four others play as survivors, trying to evade the killer and escape the playing area. Each killer and survivor has unique abilities, creating a dynamic play experience where strategy and adaptability are key. The game is set in dark, eerie environments that contribute to its tense atmosphere. Over the years, "Dead by Daylight" has expanded its universe with numerous DLCs, incorporating a variety of original and iconic characters from various horror franchises such as "Halloween," "Stranger Things," and "Saw." This inclusion adds a rich diversity to the game and enhances the overall experience with familiar horror themes and new mechanics. The game's success is driven by its intense, unpredictable gameplay and its ability to foster both cooperative and competitive play among users.
