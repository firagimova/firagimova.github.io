Fallout 76

Release year: 2018

Publisher: Bethesda Softworks

Genre: open world, multiplayer

Rate(Steam): 7/10

"Fallout 76" is an online multiplayer role-playing game developed by Bethesda Game Studios, set in the expansive post-apocalyptic world of the Fallout series. Released as the series' first multiplayer game, "Fallout 76" explores the landscape of West Virginia in an alternate timeline shortly after a nuclear war. The game emphasizes survival mechanics, resource gathering, and base-building, within a world populated by other players. Despite its rocky launch and initial criticism over technical issues and game design choices, subsequent updates and expansions have significantly improved the experience. These updates introduced new quests, NPCs, and a more traditional Fallout narrative experience. "Fallout 76" offers a unique take on the series by combining familiar RPG elements with new multiplayer dynamics, set against the backdrop of a richly detailed and vast post-nuclear American landscape.
