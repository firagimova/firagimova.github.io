DOOM

Release year: 1993

Publisher: id Software

Genre: FPS

Rate(Steam): 10/10

"DOOM" is an iconic first-person shooter video game that revolutionized the genre with its fast-paced action and advanced graphics when it was first released by id Software in 1993. The game's premise is simple yet thrilling: players take on the role of an unnamed space marine, popularly known as Doomguy, who battles demonic forces from Hell unleashed by a failed scientific experiment on the Martian moons. With its intense gameplay, players navigate labyrinthine levels, utilizing an arsenal of powerful weapons to combat a variety of demons. DOOM's legacy lies not only in its adrenaline-pumping gameplay and innovative 3D graphics but also in its wide modding community and introduction of deathmatch modes, setting the foundation for many future multiplayer gaming experiences. The game is acclaimed for its groundbreaking approach and remains a beloved classic in the gaming community, influencing countless other games in the shooter genre.
